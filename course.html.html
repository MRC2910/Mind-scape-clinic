MYTHSBUSTERS

Myth
Talking about suicide will encourage someone to attempt suiciede

Facts
Talking abiut suicide drives communication. This allows them to share their feelings and fears, which encourages someone to live.

Myth
If someone attempts suicide, they are just seeking attention.

Facts
Suicide attempts must be viewed as the intent of the person to die. The attention that we give to them may save their lives.

MYTHSBUSTERS

Myth
A consultation and therapy that comes from the profesionnal psychiatrist is the only effective intervention for suicide.

Facts
All the people who interact with them can help them by way of emotional support. The therapuetic interventions also rely heavily on the providing network.

Myth
They are constantly considered suicidal as they attempt to take their own life.

Facts
Suicidal thoughts may rebound but they are not considered permanent and they can still continue their own life.
